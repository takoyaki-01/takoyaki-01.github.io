About Me:

Photography has been a passion of mine ever since I've begun roaming this Earth. Combine that with my love for nature, and we get landscape photography. For as long as I can remember, I've always enjoyed being alone out in the wilderness and taking in the beauty of the planet. That, and people seem to always run away from me anyways, as if they'd seen a ghost. I wonder what terrifies them so much.

Whenever I pick up the camera and look through the viewfinder, I often find myself enamored by what I see before me. Everything else is gone; it's just me and the world. Sometimes, it feels as if I had ascended to a completely different, heavenly world. I feel free.

My goal for the future is to continue documenting the planet and sharing my photos with the world... and hopefully I'll also one day figure out why people keep running away.

Other things that I do include:

  • Wildlife photography
  • Portrait photography
  • Aerial drone footage
  • And lots more
